Understanding Sets

What are sets?

Sets, scripts and sequences. These are some buzz words you’ll hear used by OnlyFans creators and agencies a lot, and they matter, they are the cornerstone of earning money through PPV.
 
Think of it this way:

Set + sequence (sexting) = script

 
The SET is the 6-7-8 pieces of video content that the creator will put together that the chatters will sell to subscribers.
 
👋🏼
For the most part, this content will be sold under the illusion of being “live” content which the creator will make midway through the conversation.
 
Sets are the chatters selling a story as much as they are selling the content (or even emotion). It allows the chatter to set themselves up for success through their narrative and the content in the vault. The creator needs to understand this in detail so they can make sure the sets are created in such a way to help the chatters sell them “live”.
 
👋🏼
To make a chatters life easy, they should identify two sets of content in the vault that they will plan on selling in their shift. This will help them focus and not worry about having to try to find content each time to sell. They should be planning ahead.
